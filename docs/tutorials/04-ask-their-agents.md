# 04 · Ask their agents — work together without sending files (6 min)

**Hook (0:00–0:20).** "The old way: 'can you send me the brief?' — forty emails, nine versions, nobody knows which one is real. The new way: I ask my client's machine directly, my client asks my AI assistant directly, and NOBODY sends a file. Both of us see every question in the log."

**What you'll build.** The full two-way loop: Badis shared a folder (episode 03); now Amal shares her AI assistant back, and both work from the Ask screen.

## Steps

1. **Share an agent, not just files** (0:20): Amal → contact → *Share something* → **One of my agents** → `assistant` → look only → until I stop it → Share it. "Now Badis can question my project assistant — which answers from MY machine, MY files, MY skills. The files never leave."
2. **The Ask screen, explained** (1:10): Badis opens *Ask their agents*:
   - the chips are *what Amal shared* — each with its countdown if it has one
   - the subtitle: "only what they shared · they see every ask"
   - the placeholder adapts: an AI agent invites plain language, a folder suggests `read brief.txt`
3. **A real working session** (1:50): Badis: *"where are we on the checkout redesign?"* → Amal's assistant answers from her working files. Amal: *"read brief.txt"* on Badis's folder → the brief. Split screen. "Work is flowing. Files are not."
4. **Denials are answers** (3:00): Badis asks Amal's assistant for something outside its folder → the red bubble IS the reply. "The UI never pretends. And Amal's Activity shows the denial too — no silent failures, no silent access."
5. **The wow — the conversation survives everything (3:45):** refresh the page mid-thread → the whole conversation is still there. "It's a durable log on disk, not chat froth. And notice the grey lines — that's what THEY asked YOUR agents, in the same thread. One place, both directions."
6. **Skills make the assistant smarter live** (4:30): Amal edits `status-reports.md` in her skills folder → Badis asks again → the answer follows the new format. "No deploy. The next answer just uses it."

**Recap + CTA (5:30).** "Share an agent back → ask in plain language → denials in the open → a thread that survives. You're now collaborating without attachments. Next: [do all of this from WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord, Slack, GitHub, or GitLab — episode 05](05-channels.md)."
