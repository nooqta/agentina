# 00 · Meet agentina — your agents, your machine, your rules (2 min)

> **Playlist opener.** One job: make someone who has never heard of agentina understand what it is and install it — in under three minutes, with zero jargon.

**Hook (0:00–0:15).** "This is an AI assistant that lives on MY computer, answers ME on WhatsApp, and can safely work with ONE other person — my client, my accountant, my mom — without any of us handing over a password. It's free, there's no account, and it installs in one line. Let me show you."

**What you'll see.** The 30-second install, the console, and the two things agentina is: a home for your own AI agents, and a safe line between you and one other person.

## Steps

1. **The install — all of it** (0:15):
   ```bash
   npx agentina init --name "Amal"
   npx agentina start
   ```
   "No account. No signup. No cloud. It prints a web address — open it."
2. **The console tour** (0:45): the friendly screen — "Work together, safely." Point at the three ideas without clicking anything:
   - **My agents** — AI workers that live in folders on this machine.
   - **Invite someone** — connect with exactly one other person.
   - **Help & guides** — "the app teaches itself; every guide ends on a button that does the thing."
3. **The two promises** (1:15), on screen as text:
   - *Connecting shares nothing.* An invite only introduces two machines.
   - *Everything is on the record.* Every use and every denial lands in Activity — on both sides.
4. **Where this series goes** (1:40): flash the playlist — "episode 1: an assistant just for you, no second person needed. Episode 2: connect with someone. Episode 3: share one folder and watch it try to escape. Do them in order — each one is under ten minutes."

**Recap + CTA (2:00).** "One line to install, a web page to drive it, nothing shared until you say so. Next: [your own AI assistant on your own machine — episode 01](01-your-own-assistant.md)."
